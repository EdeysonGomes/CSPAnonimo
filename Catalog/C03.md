## **Competency C03 Specification**

### Competency Title

    Test automata using simulators


### Textual Description (final)

This competency involves the ability to **systematically test, analyze, and refine finite automata** using simulation tools. Learners must verify that finite automaton implementations conform to formal specifications by executing controlled simulations and analyzing observed behaviors.

Learners demonstrate this competency by:

* Executing simulations to validate state transitions and acceptance conditions.
* Interpreting discrepancies between expected and observed behavior.
* Diagnosing modeling errors and refining automata accordingly.
* Iteratively testing revised models until correctness and robustness are achieved.



### Knowledge Specification (final)

* **Finite State Machines**
* **Modeling and Simulation**
* **Problem Solving and Troubleshooting (FPK)**



### Disposition Specification (final)

* Investigative

* Responsible
* Proactive
* Creative



### Knowledge–Skill Pairing (final)

* **Finite State Machines – Apply** → simulate, execute, validate (behavior).
* **Modeling and Simulation – Apply** → test, interpret results (validate), refine models.
* **Problem Solving and Troubleshooting (FPK) – Apply** → diagnose (errors and implement corrections), correct.
