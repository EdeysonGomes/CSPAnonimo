# **Competency C05 Specification**

## Ciclo de Vida

**Write a Technical Report**



## 1. Definição Original — *CSP-Report (Ciclo 1)*

### Competency Title
    Collaborative Technical Report Writing

### Competency Description
This competency focuses on the ability to **collaboratively produce a well-structured technical report**. It requires students to **apply technical writing principles** to synthesize findings, explain methodologies, and document results in a clear, logical, and professional manner.  

To achieve this, students must:  
- **Write a structured and coherent report**, integrating contributions from multiple team members.  
- **Apply professional technical writing standards**, ensuring clarity, consistency, and logical flow.  
- **Effectively communicate technical findings**, making complex concepts accessible to both technical and non-technical audiences.  
- **Revise and refine content**, ensuring accuracy, readability, and adherence to formatting guidelines.  

This competency emphasizes **practical application of writing skills**, requiring students to produce a polished and well-organized document that reflects their collective expertise.  


### Knowledge Specification
The following knowledge area is essential for this competency:  

- **Written Communication (FPK)** – Involves the ability to **apply structured writing techniques** to explain and document the solution process effectively.  


### Disposition Specification  
To successfully achieve this competency, students must demonstrate key **behavioral attributes**, including:  

- **Collaboration** – Working effectively as a team to create a unified, well-organized document.  
- **Meticulousness** – Ensuring accuracy, clarity, and consistency in the report’s content and structure.  
- **Responsibility** – Taking ownership of assigned sections and contributing meaningfully to the final report.  



### Knowledge-Skill Pairing
This step maps **knowledge areas with corresponding skills**, ensuring a structured learning approach.

#### Mapping Knowledge to Skills
To demonstrate this competency, students must be able to:  
- **Apply** Written Communication (FPK) to **write, structure, and refine a technical report**, effectively explaining the solution process.  



#### Bloom’s Taxonomy Alignment
The **"Apply" level** of **Bloom’s Taxonomy** is the most appropriate for this competency because it requires students to:  
- **Use technical writing skills in practice**.  
- **Synthesize and communicate findings effectively**, ensuring clarity and coherence.  
- **Format, revise, and refine** the document to meet professional standards.  

By mastering this competency, students develop **practical writing skills essential for professional and academic communication**.  



#### Verb Annotation
To clarify expected learning outcomes, the following action verbs define key student activities:  

- **Apply** → Written Communication (FPK) → **Write, Structure, Revise, Refine** technical documentation.

## 2. Revisões — *CSRP-Report (Ciclo 1 – Revisão por Especialistas)*

### Síntese das críticas relevantes à C05

A revisão não identificou erros conceituais, mas apontou **ajustes de escopo e posicionamento**:

1. **Competência transversal**

   * A competência não é específica de autômatos, mas **aplicável a todas as tarefas do CSP**.

2. **Risco de redundância**

   * Elementos da C05 apareciam implicitamente em outras competências (documentação, explicação, justificativa).

3. **Necessidade de explicitação avaliativa**

   * Recomendada maior ênfase em **ações observáveis**, como estruturar, argumentar e reportar resultados.

**Resultado do CSRP (Ciclo 1):**
**C05 → Needs Refinement (scope clarification)**



## 3. Alterações Implementadas — *CSP-Adjustments*

### Mudanças declaradas

Com base no CSRP, foram realizados os seguintes ajustes:

* **Reafirmação do caráter transversal da competência**

  * C05 passa a ser entendida como **competência de suporte**, instanciada por múltiplas tarefas.

* **Refinamento textual**

  * Redução de descrições genéricas.
  * Ênfase em **documentação técnica baseada em evidências**.

* **Padronização terminológica**

  * Adoção explícita do termo **“Technical Report”** no sentido acadêmico-profissional.



### Competency Description (ajustada)

> This competency refers to the ability to **produce structured technical reports that clearly document problem context, computational models, implementation decisions, and validation results**. Emphasis is placed on clarity, coherence, and adherence to technical and academic conventions.

Students must be able to:

* Organize information using a standard technical report structure.
* Clearly describe models, assumptions, and decisions.
* Present results and validation evidence objectively.
* Use precise technical language and appropriate notation.

## 5. Versão Atual da Competência — *Estado Final*

### Competency Title

    Write a Technical Report


### Textual Description (final)

This competency involves the ability to **produce clear, structured, and technically accurate reports** that document computational problem-solving processes and results. Learners must communicate problem context, modeling choices, implementation details, and validation outcomes using appropriate technical language and standardized report structures.

Learners demonstrate this competency by:

* Structuring reports according to academic or professional conventions.
* Describing computational models and assumptions precisely.
* Reporting testing procedures and results objectively.
* Justifying design decisions using evidence and logical reasoning.



### Knowledge Specification (final)

### Disposition Specification (final)

### Knowledge–Skill Pairing (final)
